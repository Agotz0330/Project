Manufactures

Tesla Inc

One of the most recognizable companies is Tesla. Tesla has gained significant recognition for its diverse range of self-driving automobiles, notably including the Model S and Model Y, which stand out as prominent Tesla vehicles.

Nvidia

Nvidia is a well-established company that has made its mark in automated vehicle technology. Their expertise lies in advanced computing technologies and AI solutions specifically tailored for autonomous vehicles.

Waymo

Waymo, having accumulated an impressive track record of over 12 million miles driven since 2009, aims to tackle the elimination of human error, a leading cause of fatal crashes. Waymo achieves this through leveraging real-time sensor data and meticulously detailed custom maps, ensuring precise awareness of their vehicles' location on the road at all times.

Zoox

Zoox, designed with a focus on passengers rather than drivers, offers exceptional automated driving technology to guarantee a safe journey to the destination. Through the incorporation of cutting-edge cameras and radars, Zoox's vehicles possess a comprehensive 360-degree field of vision, encompassing 150 meters ahead of the passenger and even extending around corners.
